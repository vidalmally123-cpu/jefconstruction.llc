JEF Construction is a family-owned contracting business based in Albemarle, NC, led by Juan F. Gutierrez. We serve residential clients across Albemarle, Concord, and surrounding communities.

From crown molding and trim to full kitchen and bathroom remodels, our team brings precision craftsmanship and personal attention to every project — always on time, always clean, always proud of what we leave behind.

Crown Molding & Trim

  • Coffered ceiling integration
  • Shoe molding & quarter round

Kitchen Renovations

  • Cabinet installation & repair
  • Pantry reconfigurations
  • Countertop & backsplash
  • Finish trim & painting
03 ⊞

Bathroom Remodels

Complete bathroom transformations including tile, fixtures, vanities, shiplap, and custom trim details done with care.

  • Vanity & fixture installation
  • Tile & flooring
  • Shiplap & accent walls
  • Crown molding & trim

Laundry Room Renovations

Painting & Finishing
